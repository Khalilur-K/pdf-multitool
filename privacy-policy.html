Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 14, 2025

1. No Data Collection

This website processes all files client-side only. Your PDFs, images, and text are never uploaded to any server.

We use localStorage only to save your theme preference (light/dark mode).

3. No Tracking

No analytics, cookies, or tracking pixels are used.

4. Open Source Libraries

We use CDN-hosted libraries (pdf.js, PDF-Lib, jsPDF) from trusted providers.

5. Contact Form

Messages are sent via email. Your email is not stored.
